Opportunity Bank of Montana

In Collaboration With:
JTech Communications 2025
Focus:
UX/UI, Web, and Icon design
Tools:
Figma, Illustrator, Photoshop, Sketch

During my 2025 internship I collaborated alongside the design and development team at JTech. We were tasked with revamping Opportunity Bank's website for visual improvement and usability. I contributed by pitching to the client a card/widget system which allows them to utilize their content in a mobile first approach and compartmentalize information so that the content is easier to read and access. All the while producing iconography which can be seen used all across the website.
