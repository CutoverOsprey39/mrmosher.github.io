Flight IQ

Timeline: 2024-25
Focus: UX/UI, Mobile design & Branding
Tools: Figma & Adobe Suite

Flight IQ is an app that focuses on helping new and intermediate disc golf players become more literate about all aspects of the game. Whether it's mid-game tips, understanding how each of thier frisbee's fly or tracking a lost disc, this app is a tool for any player who wants to see performance improvements on and off the course.

Prototype demo

Tips & Tricks

In-app tips and tricks to help players understand the game better and improve their skills.

Disc Bag

A feature that allows players to track their disc inventory, including details about each disc's flight characteristics and performance history.

Tracker

A GPS-based feature that helps players track thier disc's location on the course, find lost discs, and analyze their performance on different throws. The physical tracker would be a adhesive patch which can be seen in the gallery below.

Personified settings

Allows players to adjust the app to thier native language and recieve data whether they are left, right, or ambidextrous throwers.
