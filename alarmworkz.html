During my 2025 internship I collaborated alongside the design and development team at JTech. For this website I was tasked with building a styled interface that matched with the Alarmworkz provided logo.

Type experimentation

Typeface conflicts

This was a rather difficult task because the typography for the logo would not work for the copy of the site which involed in refining sets of typefaces that would be on theme and work well together. Since specifics on what the client asked for had no details about the copy the design team and I kicked off a comparative elimination of typeface combinations till we arrived to at least four combinations. This gave us options to pitch to the client in case they were not happy with the team's first selection.
